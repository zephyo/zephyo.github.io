Description

It's your classic love story: Meet, marry, grow old.
But when your loved one hurts you, what do you do?
Is it better to love and be hurt, than ever love at all?

A slice-of-life, bittersweet visual novel.


History

I wanted to create a deeply personal story because I felt like those stories are the ones that move me the most, separating the really good from the good. My past few years at college have been a rollercoaster ride of new experiences, relationships, and plain old life lessons. So I thought I'd take the moments from my past few years that stayed with me the most and vent them out into a visual novel. Thus it took me about a month to make and is one of my most ambitious projects yet. I hope you enjoy my passion project!
