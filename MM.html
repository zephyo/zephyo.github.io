Description

‘goth gf’s iPhone’ airdrops you - accept or decline?
A love/horror story about life, death, & memes.
A shift in perspective can change everything.


History

missed messages was created for Ludum Dare 44, winning places for Mood and Graphics. For several weeks after, it was further developed, then released on Steam.


Features

  • 4 endings
  • 15 - 30 minutes each play-through
  • Original art and story
  • Available for Mac, Windows, Linux
